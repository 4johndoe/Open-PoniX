eToken Virtual

Token PKI Client supports the eToken Virtual line of products. This includes eToken Virtual and eToken Rescue. These are stored as files on your computer or on a mass storage device.

Tip: To obtain eToken Rescue or eToken Virtual, contact your system administrator.
